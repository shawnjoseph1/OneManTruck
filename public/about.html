About Our Trucking Company

We are a family-owned and operated trucking company that has been in business for over 50 years. We pride ourselves on providing personalized service to our customers and delivering their shipments on time and in perfect condition.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced drivers, dispatchers, and customer service representatives who are dedicated to providing the best possible service to our customers. We are always looking for talented individuals to join our team.

Our Fleet

We maintain a modern and well-maintained fleet of trucks and trailers to ensure that our customers' shipments are delivered safely and efficiently. Our fleet includes flatbeds, dry vans, refrigerated trailers, and more.
